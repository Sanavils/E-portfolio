Y2K STICKERS

I recently completed a captivating Y2K vintage-themed sticker collection using Adobe Illustrator. The project embodies the vibrant aesthetic of the turn of the millennium, featuring bold colors, pixelated graphics, and iconic Y2K motifs.


Each sticker is a visual journey through the era's unique blend of futuristic optimism and retro-futurism, showcasing my dedication to authentic design. This project not only pays homage to Y2K but also demonstrates my proficiency in creating visually compelling pieces that capture cultural influences.
